# Problem Translation Document

## Business Question

Did our product change drive a statistically significant improvement in
purchase conversion rate?

## Experimental Design

**Type:** Quasi-experimental (not a true RCT)
**Treatment assignment:** User signup date relative to a defined cutoff
**Control group:** Users who signed up before 2023-01-01
**Treatment group:** Users who signed up after 2023-01-01
**Primary metric:** Purchase conversion rate (purchases / sessions)
**Secondary metric:** Average order value

## Stated Assumptions

1. User signup date is a reasonable proxy for treatment exposure
2. No major confounding events occurred at the cutoff boundary
3. The two groups are otherwise comparable in behavior

## Limitations

This is a constructed scenario on synthetic data — not a causal claim.
The analysis demonstrates the statistical methodology correctly. A real
causal claim would require true randomized assignment.
